Introduction:

FirstTimer.ca is a video sharing website to share your experience and lessons in video format to help other FirstTimers to get things done by themselves! Like Obama said:" Yes We Can!!" The idea is that users upload their own videos of experience or information of getting things done. The video can be about "very simple" things that you do often but still may be a "difficulty" for a FirstTimer ...

Examples:

1. How to fill up your car's gas tank by yourself at an ESSO gas station.
2. How to check-in flights at Toronto Pearson International Airport Terminal 1. (sample1 | sample2)
3. How to apply for a Canadian Passport.

Motivation:

The power of internet is the power of information distribution and sharing. We want to build a website to share experience and information of how to do things in a visualized way. Like an old Chinese quote "To see is to believe". We believe these kinds of information is valuable and usable, and it can help others to get things done themselves and save their money and time. This is what we want FirstTimer.ca to become. We want it to become a platform to collect and distribute these kinds of information in video format. We think it will success, and we need your supports! Thanks!
